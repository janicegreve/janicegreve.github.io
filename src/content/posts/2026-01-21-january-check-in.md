*Ava Jones: The End and the Beginning* is out in the world. Reviews and feedback are making me very happy.

I'm now working on the next book in the series.

*Luna's Story: Finding Love.*

Release this year.

Not sure what difference it makes, just a nice date, if you know what I mean.

Next comes an infinity of time in the editing trenches.

I'll let you know how that's going, once I'm in there!
