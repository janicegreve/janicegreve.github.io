### Goodbye March!

Hello dear readers,
Has spring sprung with you? Here in Vikingland, it keeps changing it's mind!
Roll on days where I don't need my winter coat!

Anyway, March! Another busy month, getting *Luna's Story: Finding Love* ready for publishing.
Although we plan a timeframe, there's always that little voice asking if we will make it!
The answer is, yes! After more mini final edits than I'm willing to admit to, another go over with the proofreader,
and the usual imposter syndrome, it's done!
It's sitting there on Amazon, waiting! You can already preorder the e-book!

Exciting for us, we are publishing under our own imprint, too!\
'Aalborg GEMLINEA Publishing'

### THURSDAY 23RD APRIL

Both the paperback and the eBook are being released on the same day, this time.

It's been a super fun book to write. I might be a little bit in love with Adam, the MMC (male main character).
No one tell my hubby ;-)
I'm so excited to see if this book gets the same response as *Ava Jones: The End And The Beginning*,
the first of the *Aalborg Love Stories*.
All the reviews and messages have been wonderful to read. Making people feel good isn't to be sneezed at!
Pretty cool superpower, if you ask me!

I've also been out and about in Aalborg and by the fjord filming snippets. You might recognise some of the places, if you have already read Ava's Story. Check them out across my social media.

As well as getting Luna's Story ready, I've been writing the first draft of *Beth's Story: Accepting Love*,
which is a roller coaster of emotions. A little darker, but still with humor, love and a 'happy ever after.' I've passed 60,000 words of the first draft, which I expect to finish during April.

Please explore all of this website. There is the possibility to sign up for my newsletter which will start soon.
Completely free, and no plans to charge for it.

All that is left, is for me to wish you a very happy Eastertime, whether you celebrate or not.
As well as launching Luna's Story and writing Beth's Story I plan to go and film more snippets in Aalborg for my various social media platforms. It really is a lovely town; sitting on the fjord being all charming and hyggelig.

Wishing you good health and good reading,\
Janice xx
